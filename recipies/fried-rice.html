Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • Cooked white rice
  • Frozen peas and carrots
  • vegetable oil
  • Soy sauce

Instructions

  1. In a frying pan, add one tablespoon vegetable oil and frozen peas and carrots, keep on medium heat until thawed and warm

  2. Crack and add all 3 eggs to the pan, stir and scramble until eggs are fully cooked through and solid

  3. Add white rice to the pan, stir and add soy sauce to taste
