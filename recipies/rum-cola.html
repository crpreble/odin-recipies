Rum and Cola

Ingredients

  • 1-2 shots rum (depending on the severity of your sobriety)
  • Ice cubes
  • 1 7.5oz can of pepsi
  • Optional: One slice of lime, if you're into that kind of thing

Instructions

  1. Take an empty pint glas and add ice cubes

  2. Add shot(s) of rum to your ice cubes

  3. Add entire can of pepsi, swirl in glass to mix
