Jelly Toast

Ingredients

  • Your choice of bread
  • Butter
  • Fruit preserves (raspberry recommended)

Instructions

  1. Insert a piece of raw bread into the toaster

  2. Use knife to apply butter to only one side of the toasted bread (we will refer to this side as "top" from this point on)

  3. Rinse knife of excess butter, then apply fruit preserves to the "top" of your toast

  4. Enjoy your culinary masterpiece!
